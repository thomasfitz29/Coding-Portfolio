Unimart

IOS Marketplace App for College Students

Spring 2021

The Oppurtunity

Craigslist, Facebook Marketplace, LetGo, and other marketplace applications share one common flaw: a lack of safety. Our Product, Unimart, Seeks to solve that. Our plan was to work directly with uniersitys to establish secure market places that only their students can access. This coupled with a state of the art UI make Unimart an excellent alternative to a traditional marketplace app.

The Solution

How often have you been hesitent to purchase a used item on craigslist, Facebook Marketplace or LetGo but were afraid of meeting the seller due to saftey conerns. Our team of four set out to change this stigma by developing a mobile marketplace app catored to college students. We wanted to work with colleges to require a secure, university specific login, so only university students and staff can access said institutes marketplace. Additionally each user has a "legitmacy score" that tracks app usage, product selling, product purchasing, etc. Though this doesnt fix the problem entirely, it eliminates a majority of potential threats to college aged users esepecially women and minorities. Unfortunatly we didnt get the opurtunity to work with our univsity however it serves as an excellent proof of concept. This project was our Senior Design Project at Purdue Univerity, Spring 2020.

Objectives

  • Work directly with colleges for secure login

  • Develop easy to use, unique marketplace

  • Develop state if the art UI

  • Work with a team using scrum methodology for weekly deliverables
